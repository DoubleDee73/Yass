Notes Editing

Shift-Left/Right
Ctrl-Up/Down
or Sketch-H1/V1 (*)		 Move notes horizontally (beat)
Move notes vertically (pitch)
Ctrl-Left/Right
Alt-Left/Right
or Sketch-H2 (*)		 Stretch notes (left)
Stretch notes (right)
Ctrl+Alt+Shift+Left/Right
or Drag Marker		 Move remaining notes horizontally
Minus/Plus (main keyboard)
or Sketch-V2 (*)
Delete		 Split/Join note
Remove note
Enter
or Sketch-V3 (*)		 Add/Remove page break
Ctrl+Enter Add note
Ctrl+G/+Shift-G
Ctrl+Alt+G/+Shift-G		 Shift gap by 10/1000
Ctrl-M/D Multiply/Divide BPM by 2
(*) Short horizontal (H) or vertical (V) mouse drag, either uni-directional (1), or scribble forward-back (2), or forward-back-forward (3)
