Lyrics Editing

R/Shift-R
or Sketch-H3 (*)		 Roll lyrics right/left
F4
Right-Click Lyrics Area		 Edit lyrics
G/F Mark as golden/freestyle/plain
Shift+G / Shift+F Mark as Golden-Rap, Rap or Plain
Ctrl-Alt-Space / Ctrl-Alt-⌫ Add / remove space at/from end of syllable
' (apostrophe) Toggle gerund endings between “ing” and “in'”
(*) Short horizontal (H) or vertical (V) mouse drag, either uni-directional (1), or scribble forward-back (2), or forward-back-forward (3)
